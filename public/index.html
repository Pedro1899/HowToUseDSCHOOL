How to use it Digital School

Register your school

Contact us to help you in the process to create your school

Create new users

Create users as a profesor, administrator, or tutor, each of them with their own attributions.

Structure your school

Structure your school, create the grades that you need, and create a list of lessons for each of then, assign them to your teachers

Create students profile

Create the student profile, and assign him/her with your classroom and your tutor

Create activities

schedule activities and assign them to other teachers (if you are an administrator), choose if you want it to be an internal or external activity (tutors will be able to see it)

results management

Enter the results of the students, and see the results in graphs with means. You can also see the results of a specific student in general (if you are an administrator or tutor). Make projections based on your results
