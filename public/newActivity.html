Activity managament

schedule your activities. In this section you will be able to create external and internal activities, the difference between these two options is that the external activities that you create will be accessible by the tutor user, and the internal ones only by the other teachers of the school.
If you are an administrator user you can create activities for other users, once the activities for other users have been created, the activity will be displayed on the home page of the user to which it has been assigned.

You can also assign activities to yourself

Steep 1. Create Activity

once we are in the activity section, we must click the left button, you can see it in the red box





Steep 2. create the activity and assign it

In this section, you must enter all the data, and you must choose who you want to assign the activity to. You can assign the same activity to more than one teacher.



once you have all the information, click save.




Steep 3. view of the activity
You can also view the activities you have done, click on the button marked with red. You can delete your previous activities






Steep 4 display activities
When you create the activity, the users to whom the activity has been assigned, will be able to view the activity on the home page, when logging in.
