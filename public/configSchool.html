School's structure

here we can create our classrooms, and their assignments, we can also link teachers to their respective assignments. We will also configure the way in which our school will evaluate, quarterly, semester, bimonthly, Digital School adjusts to your need.

These functions will only be accessible with the administrative user

Steep 3. lets make the cycles of our school

This process is very important, since it will define how the results will be shown to be evaluated correctly, we will be able to decide the cycles of our school, this means if we will evaluate semester, or bi-monthly, etc. In our left horizontal bar, we will click on the button with the school icon, this is at the end of all the other icons



In the red box you can update the school icon, in the yellow box, you can see all the teachers who have already updated their profile picture, in the green box you can choose the school year (when classes start and when they end), In the blue box you can choose the school cycle, if it is semester, bimonthly, etc, in this case it is quarterly. In the black box you can choose the default language of the application for your school
